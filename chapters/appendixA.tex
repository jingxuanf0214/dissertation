    (A) Schematic defining geometry of antennal stimulation.     (B) Relationship between values for geometrical components. $r_{rest}$ changes the slope and shape of curves relating $X_{stim}$ to $X_{d}$ and $\theta_{stim}$. Upper asterisk represents approximate values of $r_{rest}$ from \cite{Azevedo2017-kj}, bottom asterisk is the estimate used in this study.
    
    (C) Stimulus-evoked responses in a model neuron with an extent spring (as in Figure 2.3F,G,H) and varying levels of excitability. Darker values indicate higher excitability, note the similarity for lower excitability simulations to the $FR$ to Figure 2.3E.
